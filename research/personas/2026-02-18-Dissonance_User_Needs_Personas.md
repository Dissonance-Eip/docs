## VERSION 2: User Needs & Pain Points — Personas Only

*(Sources: persona docs)* [musiclabelexecutive.md][music_enthusiast_ai_user.md][independantmusician.md][audioengineer.md][academicresearcher.md]

---

### AUDIO ENGINEER / STUDIO PRODUCER [audioengineer.md]

**Needs**
- Transparent last-step in mastering chain (drops into existing DAW workflow)
- Technical controls and metering (LUFS, peak, spectrogram)
- QA tools to verify no compromise to client deliverables

**Pain Points**
- Fear of client sounds being cloned to undercut professional work
- Frustration with "black box" tools that hide their process

---

### INDEPENDENT MUSICIAN [independantmusician.md]

**Needs**
- Simple one-click protection before uploads
- Presets like "platform-safe master" or "batch all"
- Clear, non-technical explanations of what the tool does

**Pain Points**
- Fear of AI remixes and style theft diluting artistic identity
- Lack of time/technical knowledge for complicated security measures

---

### MUSIC LABEL EXECUTIVE [musiclabelexecutive.md]

**Needs**
- Catalog-scale bulk processing with reporting
- Integration with asset management and delivery pipelines
- Evidence for legal/policy teams (audit logs, summaries)

**Pain Points**
- High-value catalog exposure to uncontrolled scraping
- Difficulty coordinating protection across artists, releases, territories

---

### MUSIC ENTHUSIAST / ETHICAL AI USER [music_enthusiast_ai_user.md]

**Needs**
- Transparent, explainable controls
- "Learning mode" showing how perturbations affect AI
- Community features (shared settings, educational content)

**Pain Points**
- Discomfort with opaque systems claiming to be ethical
- Tension between experimenting with AI and not exploiting artists

---

### ACADEMIC AI/AUDIO RESEARCHER [academicresearcher.md]

**Needs**
- Configurable, inspectable pipelines with parameter access
- Logs and evaluation metrics for reproducible experiments
- Clear licensing and ethical framing for publication

**Pain Points**
- Lack of open, controllable adversarial audio tools
- Concern about misuse if tools are too powerful without safeguards
