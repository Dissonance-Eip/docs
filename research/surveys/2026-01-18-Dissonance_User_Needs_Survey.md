## VERSION 1: User Needs & Pain Points — Survey Only

*(Source: raw survey CSV)* [2025-11-25-Dissonance_GF_Survey_Results.csv]

---

### NEEDS

**Protection & Control**
- Pre-emptive protection before harm occurs (most haven't experienced AI copies yet but are very worried)
- Preserve artistic identity and creative ownership
- Alignment with ethical values (consent, transparency, respect for artists)

**Workflow & Usability**
- "Set and forget" batch processing — run all uploads through it automatically
- High perceptual transparency — alterations must be undetectable
- Simple UX with minimal configuration — "upload → process → export"
- A/B comparison tool to verify no audible difference

**Compatibility & Effectiveness**
- Works with lossy formats (MP3) and survives streaming platform compression
- Actually hinders AI training, not just symbolic protection
- Multiple deployment options: desktop app, website, DAW plugin/VST

**Trust & Adoption**
- Industry recognition and legal backing
- Sustainable pricing (no subscriptions preferred)

---

### PAIN POINTS

- **Invisible exploitation** — AI trains on music without consent or visibility
- **Income and relevance anxiety** — fear of being displaced by cheaper AI clones
- **Platform hostility** — worry about shadowbanning for using anti-AI tools
- **Arms race fatigue** — AI will adapt, feels like endless cat-and-mouse
- **Friction in current options** — existing tools are complex, time-consuming, or degrade audio
