Independent Musician
=====================

Negative Trends:
 - Growing prevalence of unauthorized AI remixes and content theft
 - Digital piracy affecting revenue streams

Headaches:
 - Constant battle to protect original work from being exploited by AI tools
 - Struggling to maintain control over creative content distributed online

Fears:
 - That his music is reinterpreted or misrepresented by AI tools, diluting his artistic vision
 - Losing income as AI-generated music floods the market

Needs:
 - Robust tool to safeguard his audio files without compromising his art
 - Assurance that his creations remain authentic and under his control

Positive Trends:
 - Increasing awareness about intellectual property rights
 - Growing community that values original artistry and ethical AI use

Opportunities:
 - Leverage Dissonance to protect and authenticate his music
 - Join a network of artists advocating for ethical AI practices and content protection

Hopes:
 - Maintain complete control over his musical creations
 - Ensure his work is experienced as intended
 - inspire others to respect artistic integrity in the age of AI
